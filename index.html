Who Am I?

I'm a passionate front-end developer and UI designer who discovered my calling through an unconventional path. While I initially studied nursing, my heart led me to web development. I specialize in creating interfaces that are both visually appealing and highly functional, always keeping the end user's needs at the center of the design process.
